Our Story

Just a stone’s throw from Watford, Cloud Café began with a simple yet noble quest: to serve top-notch coffee in a space that feels as welcoming as a warm cuppa on a rainy day. From the moment we opened our doors, we’ve been all about quality, community, and making every visit feel like a proper treat.

Our beans? Handpicked from local roasters with more care than your mum puts into her Sunday roast. Our vibe? Cosier than a pair of woolly socks fresh out of the tumble dryer. Whether you’re popping in for your daily caffeine fix or escaping the chaos of the world, we’re here to brighten your day – one cracking cup at a time.
